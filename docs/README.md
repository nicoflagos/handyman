# Handyman - Business & Policy Document Pack (Drafts)

These documents are **draft templates** for launching Handyman in **Lagos, Nigeria** with a **licensed payments partner** that holds customer funds and performs regulated wallet/escrow functions.

These drafts do **not** replace professional legal, tax, compliance, or accounting advice. Before public launch, have a qualified Nigerian lawyer and accountant review and tailor them to your company name, structure, and partner contract.

## How to use
- Replace every `[PLACEHOLDER]` with your real business information.
- Keep one source of truth for each policy and update the **Effective Date** when you change terms.
- Give the payments partner the docs in `docs/payments/` and `docs/security/`.
- Use `TERMS.md`, `PRIVACY.md`, and `PROVIDER_AGREEMENT.md` as your user-facing agreements inside the app.

## Folder map
- `docs/business/` - registration checklists and company details template
- `docs/governance/` - cap table, shareholder agreement draft, and board resolutions templates
- `docs/legal/` - acceptable use, cancellation/refund, and dispute resolution policies
- `docs/kyc/` - KYC policy, ID handling/retention, verification SOP, and appeal process
- `docs/payments/` - wallet/escrow flow description, fees, settlement/payout, chargeback/refund, AML/CTF summary
- `docs/security/` - incident response, breach notifications, access control, audit logs
- `docs/ops/` - support & provider onboarding SOPs, no-show, quality policy, and SLAs
- `docs/finance/` - revenue model, commission, reconciliation, monthly reporting, tax/VAT notes
