# No-Show & Late Arrival Policy (Draft)

**Effective Date:** [DATE]

## 1) Customer no-show
If the handyman arrives and customer is unreachable:
- handyman marks `arrived`
- handyman contacts customer via in-order chat
- wait period: [X minutes]
- support can be contacted for resolution

## 2) Handyman no-show
If a handyman accepts and fails to arrive:
- customer can cancel before start/escrow funding
- repeated no-shows result in reduced visibility or suspension

## 3) Late arrival expectations
Handymen communicate delays via chat. Repeated late arrivals impact marketplace ranking.
