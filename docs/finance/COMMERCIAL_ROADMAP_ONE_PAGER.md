# Handyman — Commercial Roadmap (One Pager) (Draft)

**Launch:** April 2026 (Lagos, Nigeria)  
**Monthly growth budget:** ₦800,000  
**Priority categories:** Plumbing, Electrician, Hair Stylist, AC Tech, Nail Tech, General Errands, Cleaning  
**B2B:** Included from Phase 1

## Strategy
Two-sided marketplace with trust controls (escrow, verification codes, job photos, ratings) to improve reliability and reduce disputes.

## Revenue model
Platform fee = 5% paid by customer + 5% deducted from handyman payout (10% take rate on service fee per completed job).

## North Star Metric
Completed orders/week in Lagos with strong completion rate and repeat usage.

## Phase targets
**Phase 1 (April, Weeks 1–4): Pilot + proof**
- 40–60 handymen onboarded; 60% active
- 250–400 orders created; 150–250 completed
- 50% accepted within 2 hours; 80% accepted within 24 hours
- ≥60% completion; <5% disputes; 10–15% repeat baseline
- 2–5 B2B pilots live

**Phase 2 (May, Weeks 5–8): Reliability + retention**
- 80–120 onboarded; ≥70% verified; ≥60% active
- 500–700 created; 300–450 completed
- 60% accepted within 2 hours; 65–75% completion; 15–25% repeat
- 6–10 B2B accounts

**Phase 3 (June–July, Months 3–4): Scale Lagos + B2B expansion**
- 1,000+ completed orders/month
- 10–20 active B2B accounts
- Support cost per order decreases month-over-month

## Execution (Phase 1 summary)
- Launch in 2–3 Local Councils; expand after density proves
- Weekly handyman onboarding + 24–48h verification SLA
- Customer acquisition: estates/WhatsApp groups, flyers, micro-influencers, small paid social tests
- Price guidance ranges by category to speed negotiations

## B2B track (starts Phase 1)
- Segments: estates/property managers + SMEs (salons/offices/restaurants)
- Offer: recurring maintenance with reporting (jobs, spend, ratings)

## Budget allocation (₦800k/month)
- ₦250k onboarding + incentives
- ₦250k content + distribution
- ₦150k paid social tests
- ₦100k B2B outreach
- ₦50k contingency
