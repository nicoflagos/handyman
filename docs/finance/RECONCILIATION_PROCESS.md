# Reconciliation Process (Draft)

**Effective Date:** [DATE]

## 1) Purpose
Ensure platform ledger matches payments partner settlement reports.

## 2) Daily reconciliation (suggested)
1) Export partner transaction report (top-ups, holds, releases, refunds)
2) Export platform ledger summary
3) Match by transaction reference/order ID
4) Investigate mismatches and correct ledger entries

## 3) Monthly reconciliation
- confirm fee totals
- confirm payout totals
- confirm chargebacks/refunds
- produce management summary
