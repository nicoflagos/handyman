# Shareholders Agreement (Short Form) - Draft Template

**Company:** [COMPANY_LEGAL_NAME]  
**Effective Date:** [DATE]  
**Parties:** The shareholders listed in Schedule A.

This is a draft template intended to be reviewed and finalized by Nigerian counsel.

## 1) Definitions
- "Shares" means shares issued by the Company.
- "Board" means the directors of the Company.

## 2) Share capital and ownership
2.1 The ownership of the Company is set out in **Schedule A (Cap Table)**.  
2.2 No shareholder issues, transfers, or encumbers shares except as permitted by this Agreement.

## 3) Board and management
3.1 The Company appoints the Board members listed in Schedule B.  
3.2 Reserved matters require shareholder approval as listed in Section 6.

## 4) Founder obligations (if applicable)
4.1 Founders must devote reasonable time and attention to Company operations.  
4.2 Founders must protect Company IP and confidential information.

## 5) Transfers and pre-emption
5.1 A shareholder must offer shares to existing shareholders pro-rata before selling to a third party.  
5.2 The Company can require a buyer to sign this Agreement as a condition of transfer.

## 6) Reserved matters (examples)
Any of the following require approval of [THRESHOLD]% of shareholders:
- issuing new shares or changing share rights
- incurring debt above [AMOUNT]
- entering a merger/acquisition, liquidation, or sale of material assets
- changing business model related to regulated financial services without partner approval

## 7) Confidentiality
Shareholders must keep Company confidential information confidential.

## 8) Dispute resolution
Disputes between shareholders follow the process in `docs/legal/DISPUTE_RESOLUTION_POLICY.md` or a dedicated arbitration clause inserted by counsel.

## 9) Governing law
Federal Republic of Nigeria; venue Lagos State, Nigeria.
