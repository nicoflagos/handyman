# Dispute Resolution Policy (Draft)

**Effective Date:** [DATE]

## 1) Purpose
Handyman provides a structured process to resolve disputes between customers and handymen.

## 2) What can be disputed
- service not delivered or incomplete delivery
- unacceptable conduct
- disagreements about scope of work and service fee (where evidenced in chat)
- order start/completion disagreements (codes, arrival, photos)

## 3) How to raise a dispute
The complaining party submits:
- order ID
- issue category
- short description
- evidence (chat screenshots, photos, timeline events)

## 4) Resolution process
4.1 A support agent acknowledges the dispute within [X] business hours.  
4.2 Support reviews order events (accepted/arrived/start/complete), fee confirmation, and photos.  
4.3 Support requests statements from both parties within [X] hours.  
4.4 Support issues a decision:
- uphold release to handyman
- reverse/adjust release (if partner supports and evidence warrants)
- ban/suspend user for misconduct or fraud

## 5) Escalation
If either party disputes the decision, the case escalates to a senior reviewer within [X] business days.

## 6) Legal
This policy does not remove legal rights. Governing law is Nigeria; venue Lagos State, subject to applicable law and any arbitration clause inserted in your final Terms.
