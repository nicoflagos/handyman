# Refund & Cancellation Policy (Draft)

**Effective Date:** [DATE]

This policy explains how cancellation and refunds work on Handyman. It aligns with the current in-app workflow:
`requested → accepted → arrived → in_progress → completed` with escrow funding at start.

## 1) Definitions
- "Escrow funded" means the customer’s wallet has been debited and the funds are held pending completion.
- "Service Fee" excludes materials unless agreed otherwise.
- "Platform fee" is **5% of Service Fee** charged to the customer and **5% of Service Fee** charged to the handyman (deducted from payout).

## 2) Customer cancellation
2.1 Customer can cancel:
- while `requested` (no handyman assigned): allowed
- while `accepted` or `arrived` but **before escrow is funded**: allowed, and the handyman is notified
2.2 Customer cannot cancel after `in_progress` starts.

## 3) Handyman decline
3.1 Handyman can decline:
- while `accepted` or `arrived` but **before escrow is funded**: allowed
3.2 When declined, the order returns to `requested` and becomes available in the marketplace again.
3.3 Handyman cannot decline after `in_progress` starts.

## 4) Refunds
4.1 If escrow is not funded, there is no wallet debit and no refund is needed.  
4.2 If escrow was funded and the platform or partner reverses the debit, the platform records a refund entry in the user transaction history.

## 5) Materials and extra costs
The platform does not automatically refund materials cost because materials are outside the Service Fee unless the parties documented materials inside the order chat and paid through approved methods.

## 6) Disputes
Disputes follow `docs/legal/DISPUTE_RESOLUTION_POLICY.md`.
