# Acceptable Use & Code of Conduct Policy

**Effective Date:** [DATE]  
**Applies to:** Customers, Handymen, Admins

## 1) Purpose
This policy sets the mandatory rules for using the Handyman platform and interacting with other users.

## 2) Required conduct
You must:
- provide accurate identity and contact information
- communicate respectfully and professionally
- use the in-app workflow (chat, fee confirmation, start/completion codes)
- follow safety and property rules at job locations

## 3) Prohibited conduct
You must not:
- harass, threaten, discriminate, or intimidate any user
- request or share verification codes with third parties
- bypass the platform’s payment flow (e.g., request cash transfers to avoid escrow/fees)
- post illegal content, explicit content, or infringing content
- attempt to exploit, reverse engineer, scrape, or attack the platform
- create multiple accounts to evade enforcement or bans

## 4) Fraud prevention rules
The platform blocks or restricts accounts that:
- reuse phone/address/KYC information already used by another account (where enforced by the platform)
- submit forged IDs or suspicious verification images
- attempt to manipulate ratings, orders, or wallet balances

## 5) Enforcement
Handyman can warn, suspend, or terminate accounts for violations. Serious violations can be reported to law enforcement.
