# Business Registration Checklist (Nigeria) - Draft

**Purpose:** A checklist you can share with a lawyer/agent to register the business and prepare the basic filings to operate in Lagos, Nigeria.

**Owner:** [COMPANY_NAME]  
**Last updated:** 2026-03-06

## 1) Decide legal structure
- [ ] Business Name (BN) or Company (Ltd)
- [ ] Name reservation: 2–3 options (e.g., "Handyman Nigeria Ltd", etc.)
- [ ] Share structure: authorized share capital, share classes (if any)
- [ ] Directors/Shareholders details (IDs, address, DOB)

## 2) CAC registration deliverables (what you should receive)
- [ ] CAC Certificate of Incorporation / Registration
- [ ] CAC status report / company extract (if needed)
- [ ] Memorandum & Articles of Association (MEMART) (for companies)
- [ ] Form details (registered address, directors, shareholding)

## 3) Tax identity (FIRS)
- [ ] Tax Identification Number (TIN)
- [ ] Company email and phone on record
- [ ] VAT registration decision (ask accountant)

## 4) Banking & payments partner onboarding
- [ ] Corporate bank account (or account required by partner)
- [ ] Signed agreement with licensed partner (wallet/escrow holder)
- [ ] KYC/KYB documents requested by partner
- [ ] Settlement bank accounts for payouts and fees

## 5) Operational readiness docs
- [ ] Terms & Conditions (`TERMS.md`)
- [ ] Privacy Policy (`PRIVACY.md`)
- [ ] Provider Agreement (`PROVIDER_AGREEMENT.md`)
- [ ] Refund/Cancellation Policy (`docs/legal/REFUND_CANCELLATION_POLICY.md`)
- [ ] Dispute Resolution Policy (`docs/legal/DISPUTE_RESOLUTION_POLICY.md`)
- [ ] KYC + ID handling policies (`docs/kyc/`)

## 6) Minimum compliance checklist (practical)
- [ ] Assign a compliance contact: [NAME, ROLE, EMAIL]
- [ ] Document who can access ID images and why (`docs/security/ACCESS_CONTROL_POLICY.md`)
- [ ] Set data retention periods for IDs and wallet records (`docs/kyc/ID_HANDLING_RETENTION_POLICY.md`)
- [ ] Create an incident response contact chain (`docs/security/INCIDENT_RESPONSE_PLAN.md`)
