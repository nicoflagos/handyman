# Fee Schedule (Draft)

**Effective Date:** [DATE]

## Platform fees
- Customer platform fee: **5% of Service Fee**
- Handyman platform fee: **5% of Service Fee** (deducted from handyman payout)

## Example
Service Fee = NGN 10,000
- Customer pays: 10,000 + 5% (500) = NGN 10,500 (held in escrow at start)
- Handyman receives: 10,000 - 5% (500) = NGN 9,500 on completion
- Platform earns: NGN 1,000 total fees

## Notes
- Materials are excluded from Service Fee unless parties agree otherwise in writing in chat and through approved payment methods.
