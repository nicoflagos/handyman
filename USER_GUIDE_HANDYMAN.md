# Handyman — Handyman User Guide

**Last updated:** 2026-03-05  
This guide explains how to use Handyman as a **Handyman** (profile setup, verification, marketplace, accepting jobs, arrival, start/complete flow, payouts, ratings).

---

## 1) Create an account (Register)
1. Open the Handyman app/site.
2. Tap **Register**.
3. Fill in:
   - First name and last name
   - Phone number
   - Gender
   - Email
   - Password
4. Under **Account type**, select **Handyman**.
5. Tick the checkbox confirming you have read and agree to:
   - Terms & Conditions
   - Privacy Policy
6. Tap **Register**.

After registering, the app redirects you to **Login**.

---

## 2) Login and Dashboard
After login, your Dashboard shows:
- Your profile summary
- Your wallet balance
- Your aggregate rating as a handyman
- Your current jobs (orders assigned to you)
- Marketplace section (new jobs posted in your area)
- Transaction history

---

## 3) Update your Handyman profile (Provider settings)
1. Open **Handyman settings / Provider settings** from your dashboard.
2. Fill in your service location and profile details (as available in the form).
3. Upload:
   - Profile picture (recommended)
   - Work sample images (tiles)
   - ID image for verification

---

## 4) Verification badge (how to become Verified)
You become “Verified” on the Platform when you provide:
- address
- ID type
- valid ID number for the selected ID type
- ID image upload

Once complete, the app shows a **Verified** badge in your profile and in orders.

---

## 5) Marketplace: finding new jobs
Marketplace shows orders that are:
- `requested`
- not yet assigned to a handyman
- posted in the relevant location filters (country/state/LGA/local council)

To accept a job:
1. Open the order from Marketplace.
2. Review details (location, description, customer job photos, service fee).
3. Tap **Accept job**.

After acceptance, the order is assigned to you and no longer appears for other handymen.

---

## 6) After accepting: chat and price confirmation
After acceptance:
- You can use in-order **Chat** to clarify details and negotiate.

### Confirm service fee
Before you can start the job:
1. Ensure the customer’s service fee is correct.
2. Tap **Confirm service fee**.

Once confirmed:
- the customer cannot edit the fee anymore.

---

## 7) Mark “Arrived”
When you get to the customer’s location:
1. Open the order.
2. Tap **Mark arrived**.

The customer receives a notification that you arrived.

---

## 8) Start the job (Start code + Before photo)
To start a job you must:
- have confirmed the service fee
- collect the **Start code** from the customer
- upload a **Before image**

Steps:
1. Ask the customer for the **Start code** (only when you’re ready to begin).
2. Enter the code in the order.
3. Upload a **Before image**.
4. Tap **Start**.

When you start:
- the order becomes **In progress**
- the customer’s wallet is debited and funds are held in **escrow**

---

## 9) Complete the job (Completion code + After photo)
To complete you must:
- collect the **Completion code** from the customer
- upload an **After image**

Steps:
1. Ask the customer for the completion code after work is done.
2. Enter the completion code.
3. Upload an **After image**.
4. Tap **Complete**.

When you complete:
- the order becomes **Completed**
- escrow is released and your wallet is credited (minus platform fee)

---

## 10) Your wallet and payouts
- Your wallet balance increases when jobs are completed.
- Your transaction history shows inflows and outflows (including platform fees and payouts).

---

## 11) Decline a job
You can decline only:
- while the job is **Accepted** or **Arrived**
- before the job starts (before escrow is funded)

Declining returns the job to the marketplace so another handyman can accept it.

---

## 12) Ratings (rate the customer)
After completion:
1. Open the completed order.
2. Rate the customer (1–5 stars).
3. Submit.

Your rating helps identify reliable customers.

---

## 13) Work samples (portfolio)
Work samples are displayed as tiles on your dashboard/profile.

Use them to increase acceptance:
- upload clear photos of past work
- avoid blurred images
- upload consistent categories of work you offer

---

## 14) Rules and best practices
- Do not start without the customer’s Start code.
- Do not complete without the customer’s Completion code.
- Upload clear before/after images.
- Communicate delays in chat.
- Do not request payments outside the Platform.
