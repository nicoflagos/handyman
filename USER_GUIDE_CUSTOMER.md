# Handyman — Customer User Guide

**Last updated:** 2026-03-05  
This guide explains how to use Handyman as a **Customer** (book services, manage orders, payments, chat, ratings).

---

## 1) Create an account (Register)
1. Open the Handyman app/site.
2. Tap **Register**.
3. Fill in:
   - First name and last name
   - Phone number
   - Gender
   - Email
   - Password
4. Under **Account type**, select **Customer**.
5. Tick the checkbox confirming you have read and agree to:
   - Terms & Conditions
   - Privacy Policy
6. Tap **Register**.

After registering, the app redirects you to **Login**.

---

## 2) Login
1. Tap **Login**.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Tap **Login**.

You will land on your **Dashboard**.

---

## 3) Your Dashboard (Customer)
On your Dashboard you can:
- View your **wallet balance**
- Create a new booking/order
- See your recent orders and their current status
- View your transaction history
- View your rating as a customer (aggregate)

---

## 4) Add your profile picture (optional)
1. Go to **Dashboard**.
2. In your profile area, upload a profile picture.

This helps the handyman recognize you and increases trust.

---

## 5) Book a service (Create an Order)
1. From Dashboard, tap **Book service** (or **Create order**).
2. Select the **Service** category.
3. Enter:
   - **Title** (short summary)
   - **Description** (full details)
4. Set your **Location**:
   - State
   - LGA
   - Local Council
   - Street address (type it)
5. Set the **Service fee** (labour only).
   - Materials are excluded unless you agree separately with the handyman.
6. (Optional) Choose preferred **date** and **time**.
7. (Optional) Upload up to **2 job photos**.
8. Tap **Submit request**.

### Important: wallet requirement
The app checks you can afford:
- **Service fee + platform fee**
If your wallet is too low, top up first.

---

## 6) Order statuses (what they mean)
Your order moves through these statuses:
1. **Requested** — you posted the job, waiting for a handyman to accept.
2. **Accepted** — a handyman accepted your job.
3. **Arrived** — the handyman has arrived at your location (before starting).
4. **In progress** — the job has started; escrow is funded (money held).
5. **Completed** — the handyman completed the job; escrow is released to the handyman.

You may also see:
- **Canceled** — you canceled the order before the job started, or admin canceled.

---

## 7) Push notifications
If you allow notifications, you will get alerts when:
- A handyman accepts your order
- A handyman marks “arrived”
- The job starts (escrow funded)
- The job completes (escrow released)
- Price is confirmed/updated
- You receive a chat message

---

## 8) Chat with the handyman (price negotiation)
After a handyman accepts your order, a chat becomes available inside the order.

Use chat to:
- Explain details
- Agree on scope
- Agree the correct service fee

### Service fee editing rule
- You can edit the service fee **only until the handyman confirms price**.
- After the handyman confirms, the fee is locked.

---

## 9) Start code and completion code (very important)
When you create an order, you receive two codes:
- **Start code**
- **Completion code**

### How to use them safely
- Do **not** send codes to strangers.
- Only give the **Start code** when the handyman is physically present and ready to begin.
- Only give the **Completion code** when you confirm the job is done.

These codes protect you from false starts/completions.

---

## 10) Escrow (how payment works)
1. Before job start, your wallet must have enough for:
   - service fee + platform fee
2. When the handyman starts the job:
   - the app **deducts** the total from your wallet
   - the app holds the funds in **escrow**
3. When the handyman completes the job:
   - the app releases escrow to the handyman

---

## 11) Cancel an order
You can cancel only:
- while the order is **Requested**, **Accepted**, or **Arrived**
- and only **before** the job is started (before escrow funding)

To cancel:
1. Open the order.
2. Tap **Cancel**.

If the job already started, cancellation is not allowed in-app.

---

## 12) View job photos (customer + handyman proof)
Inside the order details you can view:
- Your uploaded job photos (if any)
- The handyman’s **before** image (when job starts)
- The handyman’s **after** image (on completion)

Tap a photo to open it.

---

## 13) Rate the handyman
After completion:
1. Open the completed order.
2. Select a rating (1–5 stars).
3. (Optional) Leave a short note.
4. Submit.

Ratings improve trust and help other customers.

---

## 14) Troubleshooting
### “Insufficient wallet balance”
Top up your wallet, then re-submit the order.

### “Unable to create order”
Common causes:
- missing location fields
- invalid date/time
- wallet insufficient
- network connection issues

### Not receiving notifications
Check:
- you allowed notifications
- battery optimization isn’t blocking the app
- you are logged in to the correct account
