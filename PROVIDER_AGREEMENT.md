# Handyman — Provider (Handyman) Agreement

**Effective Date:** 2026-03-04  
This Provider Agreement (the “Agreement”) applies to users who register as **Handymen** on the Handyman Platform. By accepting this Agreement, you agree to comply with it in addition to `TERMS.md` and `PRIVACY.md`.

## 1) Independent Contractor
1.1 You provide services as an independent contractor.  
1.2 You are responsible for your tools, transportation, staff, taxes, permits, and compliance with applicable laws.

## 2) Eligibility and Accurate Information
2.1 You provide accurate personal and verification information.  
2.2 You keep your profile information up to date, including service areas and availability.

## 3) Verification Requirements
3.1 To earn a “Verified” badge you must complete all required verification steps in the Platform, including:
- address
- ID type
- valid ID number
- ID image upload
3.2 You certify that your verification information is accurate and belongs to you.  
3.3 You do not upload forged, stolen, or misleading identity documents.

## 4) Professional Conduct and Safety
4.1 You treat Customers respectfully and professionally.  
4.2 You do not harass, threaten, discriminate, or engage in abusive conduct.  
4.3 You follow reasonable safety practices on-site and comply with building/estate rules.

## 5) Service Quality and Scope
5.1 You provide services consistent with the service category you accept.  
5.2 You communicate clearly about what the Service Fee covers. Materials are excluded unless explicitly agreed with the Customer.

## 6) No‑Shows, Delays, and Arrival
6.1 If you accept an Order, you commit to showing up at the agreed time or as soon as reasonably possible.  
6.2 You use the in-order chat to communicate delays.  
6.3 You set the Order status to `arrived` only when you have actually arrived at the job location.

## 7) Price Confirmation and Changes
7.1 You confirm the Service Fee in the Platform before starting.  
7.2 Once confirmed, you do not demand extra fees outside the Platform for the same scope, unless the Customer agrees to a scope change.

## 8) Start/Completion Codes and Job Photos
8.1 You request the Start Code from the Customer only when you are ready to begin work.  
8.2 You input the correct Start Code to start and upload the required before-image.  
8.3 You input the correct Completion Code to complete and upload the required after-image.  
8.4 You do not share Start/Completion Codes with anyone else.

## 9) Payments, Wallet, and Payouts
9.1 Payouts occur through the Platform Wallet according to the Platform’s escrow hold/release rules.  
9.2 You do not request Customers to bypass Platform payment flows.  
9.3 You accept that the Platform charges a Platform Fee equal to **5% of the Service Fee** to the Handyman (deducted from payout) as disclosed in the Platform.

## 10) Cancellations and Declines
10.1 You can decline a job only when the Platform allows it (before start and escrow funding).  
10.2 Repeated declines or no-shows can result in reduced visibility, suspension, or termination.

## 11) Portfolio and Content
11.1 Work samples you upload must be your own work or content you have the right to use.  
11.2 You do not upload illegal, explicit, or infringing content.

## 12) Enforcement
Handyman enforces this Agreement using Platform controls and can suspend or terminate your account for violations, repeated no-shows, fraud, document abuse, or unsafe conduct.
